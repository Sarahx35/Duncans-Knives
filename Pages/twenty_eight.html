Twenty Eight - a pattern welded integral bolster knife with a 4 1/2 inch blade.

I made this knife for a friend of my father. It is made from the second half of the billet I used
for knife number twenty seven. It has taken me eighteen months to finish as I've been busy this year.
The blade has 36 layers of 15n20 and EN45 which I forged into a round bar and twisted.
The handle is Bocote (Mexican Rosewood).
