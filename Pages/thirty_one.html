Thirty One - a integral kitchen knife forged from a file.

I made this knife for Elizabeth to use while at university. I used an old
round file that I also used for knife twent four. Forging the blade without
damaging the teeth on the file back or on the bolster was a challenge, but
I wanted it to be obvious where the steel came from.
The handle is maple with a black leather spacer.
