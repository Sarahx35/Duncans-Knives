Knife Nine-A 3 Inch Penknife

I made this penknife to my own design. I made a couple of prototypes first, one in
carboard, and one in steel. It works well, but the sharp corner projecting from the
handle isn't ideal. The blade is O1 tempered at 350F so it will hold a good edge,
and the spring is CS70 tempered at 525F, so it will be tough and flexible. The side
plates and rivets are mild steel, and the scales are Mexican Rosewood.
