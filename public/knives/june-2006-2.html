A Four Inch Sheath Knife

June 2006

I made this knife as a present for my Sister in Law in October 2006. It is made from the same 128 layer CS70 and 15n20 billet that I used for knife twelve. The handle is Mexican Rosewood with a brass guard. The blade is etched in ferric chloride, and the sheath is made for a left handed person.
