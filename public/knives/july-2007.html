A Four Inch Paring Knife

June 2007

This knife has a blade that is just over four inches. It is made from 144 layers of 15n20 and CS70 tool steels. I twisted the rounded billet gently, and then forged the billet close to the blade shape.

The guard is brass, and the handle is Honduras Walnut. I etched the blade in ferric chloride for just under one hour.
