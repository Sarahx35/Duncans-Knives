A Four Inch Sheath Knife

June 2006

I made this knife as a present for my friend Merv in August 2006. It is made from a 128 layer CS70 and 15n20 billet that I made when on a course with Mick Maxen. The handle is Zebrano wood with a brass guard. The blade is etched in ferric chloride, and the sheath is made from 3mm vegetable tanned leather.
