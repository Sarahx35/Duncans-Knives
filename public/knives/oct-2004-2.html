A 4 1/2 Inch Pattern Welded Blade

October 2004

This knife was made for my eldest daughter, and is made of two twisted bars of CS70 and mild steel with a spring steel edge forge welded on. There are a couple of major faults with this blade. The handle is Zebrano wood with a brass guard.
